Business Policy

We are committed to provide a safe and healthy working environment, and to prevent and eliminate hazards and risk which will minimize accidents, dangerous occurrence, occupational poisoning and disease in our organization.

To achieve this, we shall comply with applicable legal and other related requirements and continually monitor and improve our OSH performance and management system.
